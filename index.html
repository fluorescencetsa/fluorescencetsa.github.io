About Us

The Fluorescence Photography Competition awards showcase the great work produced by photographers all over the world today. We strive to discover talents that value the growing visual arts community, while bringing awareness to the social issues we face.

Participate

You can participate too! Every year we have a theme for your photos. This year, our theme is "Social Justice." You may submit a photo and also vote for other photos in different categories. Our judges will nominate 20 of the best photos to advance to the next step of the competition. Then, participants can vote for the three photos that they deem the best overall, best technical skill, and most creative.
