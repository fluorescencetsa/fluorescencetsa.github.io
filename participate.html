Submissions Are Open!

The Fluorescence Competition's theme for 2022 is

Social Justice

How the Competition Works

1. Participants submit photos following the regulations below.
2. Judges will select 20 finalists within a week after all photos are submitted.
3. The 20 finalists will be displayed on the Fluorescence website to be voted on by participants.
4. Each voter will choose three photos: An overall winner, a "most creative" winner, and a "best technical skill" winner.
5. The popular vote will win for each category, and the second most popular vote for the overall winner will receive the honorable mention award.

Submit Photo

Accepted forms of photography include: A single photo, panoramic, multiple exposure, negative sandwich, or photogram.

Copyright: Usage of any copyrighted material is not permitted in any photographic submission.

Submission Requirements:
- Participants must be at least 18 years old by the submission due date.
- Only one photo may be submitted.
- Each entry must be the original work of the participant.
- The submission should be related to the 2022 theme of “Social Justice.”
- The photo dimensions must be at least 640 x 960 pixels and 300 dpi in resolution.
- Accepted file formats: JPEG, JPG, and PNG. File size cannot exceed 5 MB.

Inappropriate or vulgar submissions will immediately be disqualified and participants will be removed from the competition.

All submissions are due on December 1st, 2022 at 11:59 PM EST.

Submit Photos

Awards

First Place Competition Winner: 500 dollar cash prize, first place medal and certificate, and statement feature on the website.

Honorable Mention: 200 dollar cash prize, honorable mention certificate, and feature in the Fluorescence virutal gallery.
Best Technical Skill: 200 dollar cash prize, best technical skill certificate, and feature in the Fluorescence virutal gallery.
Most Creative: 200 dollar cash prize, most creative certificate, and feature in the Fluorescence virutal gallery.

An enclosed medal, certificate, and cash prize will be mailed within two weeks. If you do not receive your award after two weeks, please contact us at contact@fluorescence.com.

Vote

Voting will begin on December 8th, 2022 at 12:00 AM EST after judges have nominated the 20 finalists.
Voting will end on December 14th, 2022 at 11:59 PM EST.
Winners will be announced in an online ceremony on December 15th, 2022 at 2:00 PM EST
Use the button below to vote for the best photos of this year’s theme, “Social Justice.”

Voting Criteria:
- When prompted, select the photo you would like to nominate for first place for each of the winner categories.
- The photo you choose should relate to this year's theme as well as fit the category in which you nominate it for.
